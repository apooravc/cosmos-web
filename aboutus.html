About Us

Who are we?

Cosmology Club of Delhi Technological University is a dynamic organization of vibrant students, Ph.D. scholars, professors, amateur astronomers; which aims to explore in the fields of Astronomy, Cosmology and art of reasoning in the domain of science.

Our driving factor

The origin, evolution, and the ultimate fate of the Universe is an interesting and challenging question, and when people work on interesting and challenging questions, they develop new tools that impact mankind in ways that are impossible to quantify.
